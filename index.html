Alphabet Incremental Help

  • Hover over an item to collect it and send it to the next spawner.
  • Every 10 items a spawner receives will cause it to generate an item.
  • When an item goes back to spawner A, it generates 1 prestige currency (P1).
  • Advancing to the next level requires 10 prestige currency (P10).
  • Collecting items generates money that can be used for upgrades.
  • Higher level items generate more money but high level upgrades are more expensive.
  • When many items are present, existing item values are increased instead of generating more.
  • Saves occur every 2 seconds and include the spawner progress and item total values.
  • Offline time generates speed up currency which is spent automatically when you return.
  • Placing the cursor so it overlaps spawner A will increase its spawn rate by 25%.
  • You can send friends the status text below the playfield to easily compare progress.
